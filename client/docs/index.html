Emotional health measuring

This website will send a notification to the students asking them to let us know about their emotional health status. Notifictions will be sent automaticlly at 11:00 AM and 3:00 PM. It's built with MEAN stack. click here to see the repo

Quick view


1. Students will get this notification at 11:00 PM and 3:00 PM, then we'll measure the avarege of their emotional health statuses.

2. when they click on the notification they'll get this form which represent the emotional health levels from 1 - 5 . (1 is the lowest)
